Frequently Asked Questions

What is Freesmart?

Freesmart is an innovative social platform that connects people with shared interests. We help you discover communities around transport, anime, karaoke, gaming, and more. Our smart matching system ensures you find the right people to connect with.

How do I join a community on Freesmart?

Simply browse our categories, chat with our AI assistants to get recommendations, and join conversations in communities that match your interests. It's completely free to get started!

Is Freesmart really free?

Yes! Freesmart is completely free to use. We believe in connecting people without barriers. Our platform is supported by optional premium features for enhanced community management.

What communities are available?

We have thriving communities for transport enthusiasts, anime fans, karaoke lovers, gamers, and many other interests. New communities are constantly being created by our users.

How does the AI assistant work?

Our intelligent assistants understand your interests and can recommend the perfect communities for you. Just chat with them about what you're passionate about, and they'll guide you to the right places.

Can I create my own community?

Absolutely! Freesmart empowers users to create and manage their own communities around any interest or hobby. Our tools make it easy to build engaging spaces for connection.

Is my privacy protected?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your personal information is protected, and you control what you share with the community. Read our privacy policy for full details.

How do I contact Freesmart support?

You can reach us through our AI assistants for immediate help, or contact our human support team through the contact form on our website.
